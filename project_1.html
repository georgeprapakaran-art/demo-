Our Story

Rooted in Passion, Roasted with Purpose

Ember & Oak began in a small garage with a second-hand roaster and an unwavering belief that coffee should be more than a morning routine—it should be a moment of connection.

We travel to farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, building relationships with growers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Every bean tells the story of its origin.
